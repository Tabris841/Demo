Here are several steps necessary to become our client:

  1. Customer must provide us with an architectural plan of the room wishing to furnish. Can be brought in person at our office or be sent by fax or e-mail.
  2. In the short term will be prepared and offer three-dimensional computer visualization tailored to the architectural plan and the specific requirements of the client.
  3. The customer should visit our office to get acquainted with the developed project and to express their comments and suggestions. If necessary, the project will be corrected. The project and computer visualization can be viewed only in the office of the company. They are not available to the customer to contract for fabrication, as are free. Development of conceptual design and computer visualization does not require customers to sign a contract with the company.
  4. Once specified final project, the client and a representative of the company signed contracts for the construction, which sets the date for delivery and installation. Upon signing the contract the customer pays an advance of 50% of the value of the article.
  5. One day prior to the date of installation, the customer is obliged to pay 45% of the value of the article.
  6. After completing the installation the customer pays the remaining 5% of the value of the article.
